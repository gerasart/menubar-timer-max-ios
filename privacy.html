Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 24, 2025

Simple Summary: MenuBar Timer is designed with privacy in mind. We don't collect, store, or share any of your personal data. Everything stays on your device.

1. Information We Collect

MenuBar Timer does not collect any personal information from users. Specifically:

  • We do not collect your name, email address, or contact information
  • We do not track your usage patterns or behavior
  • We do not collect any data about your timers or tasks
  • We do not use analytics or tracking services
  • We do not collect device identifiers or system information

2. How Your Data is Stored

All timer settings, preferences, and data are stored locally on your device using macOS's standard user preferences system. This includes:

  • Timer configurations and presets
  • App preferences and settings
  • Custom timer names and durations

This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third parties.

3. Third-Party Services

MenuBar Timer does not integrate with or use any third-party services, analytics platforms, or advertising networks.

4. Data Sharing

Since we don't collect any data, we have nothing to share. We do not:

  • Sell your information to third parties
  • Share data with advertisers
  • Provide information to data brokers
  • Transfer data to other companies

5. Permissions

MenuBar Timer may request the following system permissions:

  • Notifications: To display timer alerts and reminders
  • Accessibility (if applicable): Only if you enable advanced features that require system integration

These permissions are used solely for app functionality and do not involve data collection.

6. Data Security

Since all data remains on your device, your information is protected by:

  • macOS's built-in security features
  • Your device's encryption and security settings
  • Standard file system permissions

7. Children's Privacy

MenuBar Timer does not collect any information from users of any age. The app is safe for users under 13 years old as no data collection occurs.

8. Changes to This Policy

If we ever change our privacy practices, we will update this policy and notify users through:

  • App Store update notes
  • In-app notifications (if implemented)
  • Updates to this webpage

9. Your Rights

Since we don't collect your data, there's no personal information for you to:

  • Request access to
  • Request deletion of
  • Request correction of
  • Request portability of

You maintain full control over any local data by managing your app preferences or uninstalling the app.
